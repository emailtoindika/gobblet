Game Information

1. A player wins when they have three cups of the same color arranged vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. A player can place a big cup over a small cup of any color.
2. A player cannot place a cup over another cup of the same size (big over big or small over small).
3. New corn can be placed on the board or corn can be moved within the board.
